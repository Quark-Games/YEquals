Shortcuts
>>> General <<<
  (Command | Control) & 1/2/3 - switch between tabs
  (Command | Control) & A - show/hide axis
  (Command | Control) & G - show/hide grid system (grid function in beta)
  (Command | Control) & Shift & C - capture screenshot (save to Desktop)
  (Command | Control) & C - copy the entire line
  (Command | Control) & V - paste to replace current line
  (Command | Control) & M - minimise window
  (Command | Control) & Q - quit
>>> View <<<
  (Command | Control) & - (minus sign) - reduce scale (both x and y)
  (Command | Control) & = (equal sign) - reduce scale (both x and y)
  (Command | Control) & 0 - go back to default scale and origin
  (Command | Control) & 9 - equalise the scale x and scale y
  (Command | Control) & 8 - go back to origin
  Shift & (middle mouse) scroll - scale x and y
  (Command | Control) & mouse drag - move origin
  Mouse left click & drag - move origin
  Mouse right click & drag - adjust scale x and y according to movement
>>> Function tab <<<
  (Command | Control) & Backspace - remove a function
  (Command | Control) & Enter - add a new function
  Enter - make function visible/invisible
  Up/Down arrow - switch between functions
  Left/Right arrow - move cursor in function editor
  (Command | Control) & Left/Right - move cursor to left most or right most
>>> Variable tab <<<
  (Command | Control) & Backspace - remove a variable
  (Command | Control) & Enter - add a new variable
  Enter - make function visible/invisible
  Up/Down arrow - switch between functions
  Left/Right arrow - move cursor in function editor
  (Command | Control) & Left/Right - move cursor to left most or right most
>>> Debug <<<
  Tab - show processed python-friendly expressions
  Caps lock - toggle frame per second message
